Shopping Website for Mom

Project Overview:

The shopping website aims to provide a platform for My mom to sell her handmade crafts and vintage items. It will offer a seamless shopping experience to customers while showcasing the unique products crafted by My mom.

Intended Users of the Website:

  • Customers: Individuals interested in purchasing handmade crafts and vintage items.
  • My Mom: The site owner, responsible for managing product listings and processing orders.

Overview of the Content of the Website:

  1. Home Page: Welcomes visitors and highlights featured products, providing easy navigation to different product categories.
  2. Product Listings: Individual pages for product categories showcasing product details, descriptions, and prices.
  3. Product Details Page: Detailed information about each product, including large images, descriptions, pricing, and the option to add items to the cart.
  4. Shopping Cart: A page where users can view and manage items in their cart before proceeding to checkout.
  5. Checkout Page: A secure page where customers can enter shipping and payment information to complete their purchase.

Page Design Descriptions:

  1. Name of the Page: Home Page (index.html)
    Purpose of the Page: To provide a welcoming and informative landing page for visitors.
    Audience/Users of the Page: General visitors, potential customers.
    Content of the Page: Featured products, category links, brief descriptions, and a short introduction.
    Data Entry: No data entry on this page.
    Validations: No validations needed.
    Buttons/Links/Drop-downs: Category links.
    Actions: Clicking on category links will navigate to respective category pages.
  2. Name of the Page: Product Categories (e.g., Handmade Crafts, Vintage Items)
    Purpose of the Page: To showcase product categories and provide users with access to specific types of products.
    Audience/Users of the Page: General visitors, potential customers.
    Content of the Page: Lists of products in specific categories, brief descriptions, and product images.
    Data Entry: No data entry on this page.
    Validations: No validations needed.
    Buttons/Links/Drop-downs: Product links and navigation links.
    Actions: Clicking on product links will navigate to product details pages.
  3. Name of the Page: Product Details (e.g., Product1.html, Product2.html)
    Purpose of the Page: To provide detailed information about a specific product and enable users to add it to their cart.
    Audience/Users of the Page: General visitors, potential customers.
    Content of the Page: Product images, descriptions, pricing, and an "Add to Cart" button.
    Data Entry: Users can add items to their cart.
    Validations: Ensure that users can't add more items to the cart than available in stock.
    Buttons/Links/Drop-downs: "Add to Cart" button.
    Actions: Clicking the "Add to Cart" button will update the cart contents.
  4. Name of the Page: Shopping Cart (cart.html)
    Purpose of the Page: To allow users to view and manage items in their shopping cart before checkout.
    Audience/Users of the Page: Customers who have added items to their cart.
    Content of the Page: List of items in the cart, quantities, prices, and total cost.
    Data Entry: Users can update item quantities and remove items.
    Validations: Ensure that users can't update quantities to be less than 1 or more than available stock.
    Buttons/Links/Drop-downs: "Checkout" button and options to update quantities.
    Actions: Clicking the "Checkout" button will navigate to the checkout page.
  5. Name of the Page: Checkout Page (checkout.html)
    Purpose of the Page: To collect shipping and payment information from customers to complete the purchase.
    Audience/Users of the Page: Customers ready to make a purchase.
    Content of the Page: Form fields for shipping and payment information, order summary.
    Data Entry: Users enter shipping and payment details.
    Validations: Validate form fields for completeness and accuracy.
    Buttons/Links/Drop-downs: "Submit" button for order confirmation.
    Actions: Submitting the form will process the order and display a confirmation.
